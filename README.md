Bank-Consumer-Churn-Analysis-Dashboard
Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn patterns using a banking customer dataset. The objective is to identify the major factors influencing customer attrition and provide actionable business insights through interactive visualizations.

Objectives
Monitor overall customer churn.
Identify customer segments with higher churn.
Analyze churn based on demographics and customer behavior.
Compare churn rate against business targets.
Support data-driven customer retention strategies.
Key Insights
The dataset contains *10,000 bank customers, with approximately **2,000 customers leaving, resulting in an overall *20% customer churn rate.
Customer distribution is nearly balanced across genders, suggesting that gender alone is not a significant factor influencing churn.
The majority of customers are credit card holders, allowing comparison of retention patterns based on banking product ownership.
France has the largest customer base, followed by Germany and Spain, providing valuable regional insights into customer distribution.
Most customers own one or two banking products, while customers with three or more products represent a smaller segment.
Customers aged 41–60 years exhibit the highest churn rate, making this age group a priority for retention initiatives.
Customers with lower credit scores demonstrate a higher tendency to churn, indicating that credit score is an important risk indicator.
Customers with higher account balances represent valuable business assets, making their retention essential for long-term profitability.
The dashboard includes a Churn Rate Gauge that compares the current churn rate against a target, enabling quick performance monitoring.
Interactive filters allow users to explore churn trends across different customer segments for deeper business analysis.
Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders:

Monitor overall customer churn performance.
Identify high-risk customer segments.
Understand the impact of customer demographics and financial characteristics on churn.
Support data-driven customer retention strategies.
Improve decision-making through interactive and dynamic visualizations.
Prioritize high-value customers for targeted retention campaigns.
